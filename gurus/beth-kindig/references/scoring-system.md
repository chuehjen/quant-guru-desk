# Forward-Growth Scorecard

A quantitative ranking system distilled from Beth Kindig's published signals. Score each candidate 0-5 on eight positive factors, weight to 100, then apply penalties. Severe penalties are kill switches.

## Positive factors (0-5 each)

### 1. Revenue growth & acceleration — weight 16%
- 5: triple-digit or strongly *re-accelerating* growth
- 3: solid double-digit, stable
- 1: decelerating / single-digit
- Kindig prizes *acceleration*, not just level (Palantir "revenue acceleration via AIP"; Bloom "Q1 +130% YoY").

### 2. Gross-margin trajectory — weight 12%
- 5: margins expanding sharply with scale
- 3: stable, healthy
- 1: compressing
- "Margins expanding sharply" is a recurring green flag.

### 3. Operating leverage / GAAP path — weight 13%
- 5: clear flow-through to profit; a GAAP profitability breakthrough
- 3: improving operating margin
- 1: cash-burning with no leverage in sight
- "GAAP profitability breakthrough" = major re-rating catalyst.

### 4. Leadership & moat — weight 14%
- 5: platform standard / winner-take-most (CUDA-type moat); gaining share
- 3: strong #2 with a defensible niche
- 1: commodity/also-ran
- "Own the leader of a secular trend, early."

### 5. Bottleneck position — weight 12%
- 5: sits squarely on the *current binding constraint* of the AI buildout (e.g., power in 2026)
- 3: adjacent to the bottleneck
- 1: in an already-relieved part of the chain
- "Time to power is the variable that matters most."

### 6. Product-cycle catalyst — weight 10%
- 5: dated product transition with clear pricing power within 1-2 quarters
- 3: catalyst within a year
- 1: none identifiable
- Hopper→Blackwell→Rubin; "Blackwell priced 40% higher."

### 7. Forward-valuation attractiveness — weight 13%
- 5: forward multiple "eerily low" vs forward growth; best in the opportunity set
- 3: fair vs growth
- 1: fully priced-in / no forward upside left
- Growth-adjusted, judged against the rest of the book.

### 8. Evidence quality — weight 10%
- 5: company filings, earnings transcripts, official guidance
- 3: reputable media, sell-side research, IR
- 1: rumor, social media

**Composite = Σ(score × weight) / 5 × 100.**

## Penalty factors

| Penalty | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Accounting / governance red flag** | **KILL SWITCH — exclude** (Super Micro auditing-issue exit template) |
| Serial dilution (ATM/secondaries >> growth funding need) | −10 to −20 |
| Decelerating growth with no margin offset | −10 |
| Crowded / fully priced-in (no forward upside vs opportunity set) | −10 |
| Single-customer concentration risk | −5 to −10 |

## Rating bands

| Score | Band | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 85+ | Top conviction | 15-20% position |
| 70-84 | High conviction | 8-14% |
| 55-69 | Worth holding | 4-7% |
| <55 | Pass | Monitor only |
