## Famous trade examples

### NVDA breakout (2023 pattern — illustrative)
- **Setup**: After Oct 2022 bear market low, NVDA based for 5 months
- **Stage 2 confirmation**: Broke above 200-day MA in Jan 2023 on AI/ChatGPT catalyst
- **VCP**: Formed tight 3-contraction pattern in May 2023 (12% → 8% → 5% contractions)
- **Pivot**: ~$305 area
- **Breakout**: Earnings gap + follow-through, volume 5x average
- **Result**: $305 → $500+ in 4 months (+65%)
- **Lesson**: Fundamental catalyst (AI) + perfect VCP + Stage 2 = massive winner

### SHOP breakout pattern (classic power play)
- **Setup**: After establishing Stage 2, formed extremely tight 5-day consolidation
- **Pattern type**: Power play (< 5% range, volume -70% from average)
- **Entry**: Break above consolidation high on volume surge
- **Stop**: Below consolidation low (only 3.5% risk)
- **Result**: Immediate follow-through, 20%+ in 2 weeks
- **Lesson**: When a stock WON'T pull back, the tight pause IS the setup

### Classic VCP anatomy (generalized from Minervini's teaching)
```
Week 1-3: Rally to new high → 1st pullback: -25% (heavy volume selling)
Week 4-6: Recovery to near high → 2nd pullback: -15% (moderate volume)
Week 7-9: Recovery again → 3rd pullback: -8% (light volume — sellers exhausted)
Week 10: Tight range 3-5 days, volume -60% → PIVOT IDENTIFIED
Week 11: Break above pivot on 2x volume → BUY
Stop: Below week 10 low (7% below entry)
Target: +20% → sell half, trail rest on 20-day MA
```

## When the methodology FAILS

### Common failure modes (honest assessment)
1. **Bear market whipsaw** — breakouts fail repeatedly; stopped out 3-4 times in a row; losses accumulate. Solution: go to cash when market breadth deteriorates.
2. **Late-stage breakouts** — 4th/5th base breakouts have 50-70% failure rate. The stock is "known" and crowded.
3. **Earnings gap-down** — stock gaps below stop on earnings miss; actual loss exceeds planned risk. Solution: reduce size before earnings or exit entirely.
4. **Sector rotation** — the sector that was leading suddenly rotates; stocks break down from perfect setups. Solution: diversify across 2-3 sectors minimum.
5. **Low-ADV stocks** — thin stocks can gap through stops or have false breakouts on low volume. Solution: only trade stocks with average daily volume > $10M.

### Minervini's worst environments:
- 2000-2002 (dot-com crash): momentum completely crushed
- 2008 (financial crisis): everything correlates to the downside
- 2022 (rate hike bear): persistent Stage 4, no setups qualify for months
- Choppy sideways markets (2015-2016): breakouts fail, false starts

### His response to drawdowns:
"When I'm getting stopped out repeatedly, I don't fight it. I reduce size, go to cash, and WAIT. The market will tell me when it's ready. My job is to preserve capital so I can strike hard when conditions turn favorable."

## Teaching and philosophy

### Key principles (from his books and interviews):

1. **"Superperformance stocks share predictable characteristics BEFORE they make their big moves."** This is the basis of SEPA — the patterns repeat because human psychology repeats.

2. **"I'd rather buy a stock at $50 that's going to $100 than buy at $30 hoping it'll go to $50."** Buy strength, not weakness. The cheap stocks are cheap for a reason.

3. **"Losses are the cost of doing business. The goal is to keep them small and infrequent."** 3-4 small losses for every 1 big win still produces excellent returns if the wins are 3-5x the losses.

4. **"The stock market is a graveyard of people who were right about a stock but wrong about timing."** Being right about fundamentals doesn't save you from a 40% drawdown if you buy at the wrong stage.

5. **"Position sizing and stop-losses are not optional accessories — they ARE the strategy."** Without risk management, even a 90% win rate will eventually blow up.
